What We Add

Custom Design Additions for Any Gown

Every addition is built to match your existing gown — fabric, lace, embellishments. We source materials to match, or work with what you provide.

Sleeves & Illusion Panels

Illusion lace sleeves, full sleeves, cap sleeves, flutter sleeves, and detachable styles. Built from scratch in lace or fabric that precisely matches your gown. The most-requested addition at our studio — and the one that surprises brides most with how natural they look.

Train Additions & Extensions

Adding a cathedral, chapel, or sweep train to a gown that didn’t come with one. Extending an existing train. Converting a fixed train to detachable for the reception. Or adding a detachable overskirt for a dramatic second look.

Beading & Lace Additions

Hand-applied beading, crystal embellishments, lace appliqués, and embroidery details. Adding to existing patterns or creating new detail in areas that need more visual weight. Every bead placed by hand. Every lace motif positioned with purpose.

Back Design Changes

Lowering or reshaping the back opening. Adding a corset closure. Converting a zipper to buttons. Adding a bow, illusion panel, or keyhole detail. Your guests will see your back all night — it should be as beautiful as the front.

Detachable Elements

Detachable sleeves that come off for the reception. A removable skirt overlay. A bow or sash that changes the silhouette. Two looks, one gown — exactly as it should be for a long wedding day.

Neckline & Strap Additions

Adding straps to a strapless gown. Converting a square neck to a V or scoop. Adding a modesty panel. Adding an illusion neckline extension. Small changes that make a gown feel exactly right from the moment you put it on.

Our Signature Addition

The Illusion Sleeve.
Our Most-Requested Work.

More brides come to Margo West for custom illusion lace sleeves than for any other addition. And it’s easy to understand why — a perfectly matched illusion sleeve can completely transform a gown from strapless to sophisticated.

Each sleeve is built by hand. We source lace that matches your gown’s existing pattern — sometimes exactly, sometimes by creating a complementary pattern that reads as intentional. The result never looks added on.

  • Cap sleeves, elbow-length, or full-length options
  • Fixed or detachable attachments
  • Lace, beaded illusion, or fabric construction
  • Matched to your existing lace pattern
  • Tested at a fitting before final attachment
