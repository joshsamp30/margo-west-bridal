Gown Alterations

Found the perfect gown but the fit isn’t quite there? We alter mother of the bride dresses from any source — boutiques, department stores, online retailers, or heirloom pieces passed down through the family.

  • Taking in or letting out the bodice
  • Hemming and length adjustments
  • Sleeve modifications and additions
  • Back alterations and closures
  • Structural reshaping for fit
✦

Custom MOB Gown Design

If you have a specific vision that no off-the-rack gown can fulfill — a particular color to coordinate with the wedding party, coverage you need, a silhouette you’ve always loved — we design and build it from scratch.

  • Bespoke silhouette design
  • Color-matched fabrication
  • Custom coverage and modesty solutions
  • Matching or complementary details to bridal party
  • Multiple private fittings included
✦

Finishing & Prep

In the week before the wedding, we provide final pressing, steaming, and any last-minute adjustments so your gown arrives on the day exactly as you need it — wrinkle-free, perfectly fitted, and ready to wear.

  • Professional bridal pressing and steaming
  • Last-minute emergency fitting adjustments
  • Gown inspection and quality check
  • Storage and garment bag care instructions
