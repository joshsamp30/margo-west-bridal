We Do the Alterations
Other Designers Won’t.

At Margo West Bridal Couture, alterations aren’t just hemming and taking in seams. We specialize in dramatic, couture-level transformations.reworking entire silhouettes, adding structural elements, and redesigning gowns to match the exact vision you have in your head.

This is what sets us apart. Other ateliers say no. We say yes.

  • Personalized fit for a flattering silhouette
  • Vintage & heirloom gown revitalization
  • Custom additions. Sleeves, slits, trains, beading
  • Couture redesigns & complete transformations
  • Expert handling of lace, beadwork & delicate fabrics

Meet the Founder

40+ Years.
One Purpose.
You.

Hello Beautiful. I’m Margo West. My journey began in 1984 when a family friend asked me to restyle a vintage wedding dress for her sister. That one act of love and craft changed everything.

For more than 40 years, I’ve dedicated my life to making sure every bride feels radiant, confident, and completely herself on the most unforgettable day of her life. My sister Lisa is by my side every step of the way.

When you walk through our door on Slocum Street, you are not a number. You are the reason we do this.

Love Always, Margo West
