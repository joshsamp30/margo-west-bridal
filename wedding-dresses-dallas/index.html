Wedding Dresses in Dallas TX

Margo West is a couture atelier, not a bridal boutique. We don’t stock sample gowns or sell from a catalog — we design and build each gown specifically for the bride in front of us. If you already have a gown, we alter it with couture-level craftsmanship. If you want something created for you, we design it from scratch. Everything is done in-house, personally by Margo West.

We’re located at 1403 Slocum Street, Suite 103, Dallas TX 75207 — in the heart of the Dallas Design District. Hours are Monday through Friday 9am–5pm and Saturday–Sunday 11am–5pm. All appointments are by consultation — call (972) 918-9750 or book online.

We begin with a $100 consultation deposit, which is applied toward your total alteration or design cost.
