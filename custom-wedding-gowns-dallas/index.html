The Complete Bespoke Experience

Every custom gown at Margo West includes a full design journey — not just a measurement and a pattern. This is what you get when you say yes.

🎨

2-Hour Design Session

A private session with Margo. You bring your inspiration — photos, Pinterest boards, fabric swatches, anything. She listens, asks questions, and begins sketching your gown before you leave.

📐

Sketch Approval

A detailed sketch of your custom gown with fabric swatches attached. You approve (and can refine) before a single seam is sewn. Your vision, confirmed before we cut.

🪡

Muslin Fitting

Before any luxury fabric is cut, a muslin mock-up is built to your exact measurements. Fit is perfected at this stage. You feel the silhouette. You confirm the drape.

👗

Final Construction & Delivery

The gown is built in your chosen fabrics with multiple fittings along the way. Final delivery includes pressing, a garment bag, and care instructions. Ready 2–4 weeks before your wedding.

The Journey

From Your Vision to Your Vows

Building a bespoke gown takes time, collaboration, and artistry. Here’s how the process unfolds — beginning to end.

01

Initial Design Consultation — 2 Hours

Book your private session with Margo. Bring everything that inspires you. She listens, sketches, and defines the design direction. Fabric and embellishment options reviewed. Sketch and quote provided before you leave.

02

Sketch Approval & Fabric Selection

Margo refines the sketch based on any feedback. You approve the design and fabric selections. Specialty laces, silks, tulle, and embellishments are sourced. Production begins only after your signature approval.

03

Muslin Mock-Up & Fit Session

A full mock-up in muslin is constructed to your measurements. You wear it, move in it, and confirm the silhouette, proportions, and structure. All refinements are made before a single inch of your luxury fabric is cut.

04

Construction & Intermediate Fittings

The gown is constructed with ongoing fittings as each stage completes. Beading, lace application, embellishment work, and finishing details are applied by hand. You’re involved at every milestone.

05

Final Fitting & Delivery

The completed gown is yours to try on for the first time in full. Final minor adjustments are made on the spot. The gown is pressed, protected, and delivered in a custom garment bag — ready for your wedding day.

Custom gown timelines are 6–12 months. Booking early ensures the best timeline and fabric availability. Ready to start? Schedule your consultation today.
