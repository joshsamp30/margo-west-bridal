The Studio

Not Just a Boutique.
A Couture Atelier.

Most bridal boutiques sell dresses off the rack. Margo West Bridal Couture creates and transforms them. Since 1984, brides from across Dallas and North Texas have come to our Design District studio not just for alterations — but for the kind of craftsmanship that turns a wedding dress into a work of art.

Founder Margo West personally oversees every project in the studio. There are no junior alterations teams, no outsourced work. When you bring your gown to Margo West, you get Margo West. That’s what 40+ years of reputation is built on.

“I built this studio to honor the tradition of couture — where a garment is crafted by skilled hands for a specific person, with precision and love.”
— Margo West, Founder
