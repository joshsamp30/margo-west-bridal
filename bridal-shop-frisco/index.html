Frequently Asked Questions from Frisco Brides

Do I need to come in person for a consultation?

Yes. For alterations, we need to see you in the gown to properly assess what needs to happen. There is no substitute for an in-person fitting. The 30 minutes drive from Frisco is part of the process, and brides consistently tell us it was worth every minute.

How far in advance should I book?

For alterations, we recommend booking at least 3–4 months before your wedding date, with your final fitting 2 weeks out. For custom gowns, the lead time is 6–12 months. If your wedding is approaching, call us. We'll tell you honestly whether we can help.

Is the $100 consultation fee refundable?

The $100 deposit is applied directly to your total when you move forward with services. It's not a fee. It's a credit. It simply ensures that both of us are committed to the appointment.
